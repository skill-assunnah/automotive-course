Professional Automotive Technician Training

3-Month Advanced Program
Organized by: As Sunnah Skill Development Institute

Course Overview

This 12-week program is designed to develop skilled, job-ready automotive technicians. Combining foundational mechanical knowledge with modern diagnostic expertise, it prepares participants for careers in the automotive industry. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, real-world problem-solving, and professional work ethics, while also integrating Islamic values like safety, responsibility, and integrity.

  • Apply workshop ethics and safety
