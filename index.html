Welcome to the Automotive Course

This comprehensive course offered by As Sunnah Skill Development Institute provides hands-on training in automotive repair and maintenance.

Our curriculum covers everything from basic vehicle maintenance to advanced diagnostic techniques. You'll gain practical experience with real-world scenarios, guided by expert instructors. Topics include engine repair, electrical systems, brake systems, and more. By the end of the course, you'll be equipped to handle a wide range of automotive challenges and pursue a rewarding career.
