Course Overview

The Professional Automotive Technician Training is a rigorous, 12-week intensive program meticulously designed to develop skilled, job-ready automotive technicians. Conducted by As Sunnah Skill Development Institute, this program combines foundational mechanical knowledge with modern diagnostic expertise, ensuring participants are fully prepared for careers in the local and international automotive service industry.

Rooted in hands-on learning and real-world problem-solving, the curriculum balances technical skill-building, diagnostic confidence, and professional work ethics, producing technicians capable of servicing both conventional and modern vehicles.
