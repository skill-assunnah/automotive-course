About the Program

The Professional Automotive Technician Training course is a comprehensive 3-month program designed to equip aspiring technicians with in-depth knowledge and practical skills in modern automobile systems. Our training combines theory, hands-on sessions, and diagnostics to ensure students graduate ready to work in real-world automotive service environments.

Objectives

  • Develop core competencies in mechanical, electrical, and diagnostic automotive systems.
  • Ensure safety awareness, ethical workshop practices, and tool proficiency.
  • Prepare students for entry-level technician roles or advanced study in automotive engineering.

Who Should Enroll?

This program is ideal for:

  • Students with a passion for vehicles and technology
  • Beginners looking to enter the automotive industry
  • Workshop assistants wanting to upgrade their skills
  • Technicians aiming to specialize in diagnostics or hybrid systems

Training Highlights

  • 12 weeks of structured, hands-on learning
  • Modern tools and diagnostic equipment
  • Guidance from certified instructors
  • Final certification upon successful completion
