Weekly Lesson Plan: Module Breakdown

Duration: 12 Weeks

Weekly Schedule: Saturdayâ€“Thursday (6 days/week)

Daily Load: 8 Classes/day (approx. 45â€“50 mins each)

Total Classes/Week: 48 sessions

Week 1: Safety, Tools & Workshop Introduction

Module: 1.1 Safety and Hazard, 1.2 Tools, 1.3 Major Systems Overview

Theory (T): OHS, PPE, Workshop Ethics, Tool Identification, Basic Systems

Practical (P): PPE demo, safety drills, tool handling, tool inspection, system diagram overview

Week 2: Engine System & Basic Components

Module: 1.4 Engine System

Theory (T): SI vs CI engines, 4-stroke/2-stroke cycles, engine components, lubrication/cooling path

Practical (P): Identify engine parts, oil circulation path, cooling system inspection, measurements (bore, stroke)

Week 3: Fuel, Cooling & Lubrication System

Modules: 2.1 Fuel System, 2.2 Cooling System, 2.3 Lubrication

Theory (T): Components and functions of each system

Practical (P): Fuel filter change, coolant flush/refill, thermostat test, oil & filter change

Week 4: Starting, Ignition & Air Induction

Modules: 3.1 Starting, 3.2 Ignition, 3.3 Air Induction

Theory (T): Component roles and operational principles

Practical (P): Spark plug replacement, starter testing, air filter/throttle body servicing

Week 5: Exhaust, SI & CI Engine Tune-Up

Modules: 4.1 Exhaust, 4.2 SI Engine, 4.3 Diesel Engine Tune-Up

Theory (T): Engine stroke cycles, tune-up objectives, emissions

Practical (P): Tune-up of SI engine (timing, plug), diesel injector testing, exhaust leak check

Week 6: Powertrain & Steering Systems

Modules: 5.1 Powertrain, 5.2 Steering

Theory (T): Transmission, clutch, differential, steering types and linkages

Practical (P): Clutch adjustment, fluid check, power steering diagnosis, CV joint inspection

Week 7: Suspension, Wheel Alignment & Balancing

Modules: 6.1 Suspension, 6.2 Alignment

Theory (T): Suspension theory, camber/caster/toe angles

Practical (P): Inspect shocks, bushings, wheel balancing, alignment with tools

Week 8: Braking System & A/C Maintenance

Modules: 7.1 Brake System, 7.2 A/C

Theory (T): Brake types, ABS, A/C components and principles

Practical (P): Brake bleeding, pad replacement, A/C refrigerant check, blower service

Week 9: Electrical, EFI & Charging System

Modules: 8.1 Electrical, 8.2 EFI, 8.3 Charging

Theory (T): Lighting, sensors, EFI logic, alternator

Practical (P): Fuse replacement, multimeter testing, injector cleaning, scan tool use

Week 10: LPG/CNG System & Calibration

Modules: 9.1 LPG/CNG Install, 9.2 Calibration

Theory (T): CNG/LPG working, safety, tuning logic

Practical (P): Gas leak test, regulator setup, calibration with tool/software

Week 11: Hybrid, EV, and Advanced Electronics

Modules: 10.3 Hybrid/EV, 11.1 Electronics & Diagnostics, 11.2 ADAS

Theory (T): EV components, CAN bus, diagnostics, ADAS

Practical (P): Identify EV parts, simulate diagnostics, sensor testing, safety tools

Week 12: Final Exams & Certification

Modules: 12.1â€“12.4

Written Exam: Covering all modules

Practical: Engine, brake, electrical, EFI, A/C tasks

Oral: Troubleshooting logic, safety, diagnostic explanation

Certification: Feedback, future guidance
